Welcome to Lotus Network

Move was the foundation to Facebook’s Libra blockchain, this would have taken the company from a social media platform with billions of users to a massive, global spanning bank with a commodity basket. With such resources at its deposal, Facebook magnified the brainpower to devise Move, a Rust-based programming language. The potential was to enable people to pay for taxi’s, send tips, purchase items, all sorts, through the app plus more and in order to do that the developer language had to be security focus. In the end, Libra became Diem and Diem died as USA authorities could not accept such a big tech firm taking over the dollar. Meanwhile, Facebook has rebranded to Meta and is taking on AI. Nonetheless, the Move language has lived on and Lotus has risen.
